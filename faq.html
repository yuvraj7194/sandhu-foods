How do I track my order?

Once your order is placed, you will receive a confirmation email. You can also view your order history in the "Orders" section of our site.

What are the shipping charges?

We offer FREE Shipping on all orders above ₹500. For orders below ₹500, a flat shipping fee of ₹50 applies.

How long does delivery take?

Typically, orders are delivered within 3-5 business days depending on your location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept all major Credit/Debit cards, UPI, and Net Banking through our secure checkout.
