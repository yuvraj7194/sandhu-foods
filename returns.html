Return Policy

Since we deal in food products, we only accept returns if the product received is damaged, expired, or incorrect.

How to request a return?

Please contact our support team within 24 hours of delivery with photos of the damaged product and your Order ID.

Refund Process

Once approved, your refund will be processed to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.

Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged during transit.
