At Sandhu Foods, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information Collection

We collect your name, email, phone, and address only when you place an order. This is required for shipping and order confirmation.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used to process orders and send receipts via Email. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Local Storage

We use your browser's "Local Storage" to save your Order History locally on your device. This data stays on your computer.

4. Security

We use Formspree and EmailJS to ensure your order details are transmitted securely to our team.
